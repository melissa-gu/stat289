U.S. Mills

U.S. Mills is a packaged food products company specializing in natural , organic , and specialty cereals , cookies , and crackers . Their products are sold through supermarkets, wholesale grocers, and natural food distributors nationwide. Their brands include Uncle Sam Cereal , Erewhon , New Morning, Skinner's Raisin Bran, and, formerly, Farina .
