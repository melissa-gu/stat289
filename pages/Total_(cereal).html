Total (cereal)

Total is a range of breakfast cereals made by General Mills for the United States market. It consists of whole grain wheat flakes. Some varieties of Total supply 100% of the US Department of Agriculture's ( USDA ) recommended daily allowance for each of the following different vitamins and dietary minerals : vitamin C , calcium , iron , vitamin E , thiamin , riboflavin , niacin , vitamin B 6 , folic acid , vitamin B 12 , pantothenic acid and zinc . The cereal was launched in 1961.

Total is a breakfast cereal made by General Mills . It consists of Whole Grain Wheat Flakes. Total is one of the most popular cereals in the United States , as it supplies 100% of the US Department of Agriculture's ( USDA ) recommended daily allowance for each of the following different vitamins and minerals: Vitamin C , Calcium , Iron , Vitamin E , Thiamin , Riboflavin , Niacin , Vitamin B6 , Folic Acid , Vitamin B12 , Pantothenic Acid , and Zinc . Other varieties of Total include: Total Brown Sugar & Oats (Discontinued), Total Corn Flakes (Discontinued), Total Honey Clusters, Total Protein (Discontinued), Total Raisin Bran, Total Vanilla Yogurt and Total With Strawberries.
