Golden Crisp is a breakfast cereal made by Post Cereals . It consists of sweetened puffed wheat . The advertisements feature its mascot , an anthropomorphic cartoon bear character known as Sugar Bear , who sings the jingle , "Can't get enough of that Golden Crisp." In the 1980s, Sugar Bear could turn into "Super Bear" upon eating it, and the jingle was appended, "it's got the crunch with punch". Sugar Bear's voice was reminiscent of Bing Crosby .
