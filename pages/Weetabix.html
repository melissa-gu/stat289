Weetabix

Weetabix is a whole grain wheat breakfast cereal produced by Weetabix Limited in the United Kingdom. It comes in the form of palm-sized (approx. 9.5 cm × 5.0 cm or 4" × 2") rounded rectangle-shaped biscuits . Variants include organic and Weetabix Minis (bite-sized) versions. [1] The UK cereal is manufactured in Burton Latimer , Northamptonshire , and exported to over 80 countries. [2] Weetabix for the Canada and United States is manufactured in Cobourg , Ontario , in both organic and conventional versions.
