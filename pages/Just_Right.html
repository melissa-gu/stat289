Just Right

Just Right is the name of a breakfast cereal produced by Kellogg's . It was created in the 1980s for health-conscious, athletic Australians , who are the world's third-biggest cereal eaters. [1] The cereal is sold in the United Kingdom , Canada , Iceland , Ireland , New Zealand and Australia as of March 2008, but has been discontinued in other markets.
