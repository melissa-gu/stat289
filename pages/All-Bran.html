All-Bran is a very high- bran , high- fiber , wheat bran breakfast cereal manufactured by Kellogg's and marketed as an aid to digestive health. Due to the low levels of sodium , along with high levels of potassium , All-Bran cereal is proven to have a positive effect on blood pressure as well. One serving of All-Bran provides nearly 50% of the daily value of fiber. It is considered to be low in total fat , saturated fat , and cholesterol . It is often joked that it is popular with older ladies [ citation needed ] .
