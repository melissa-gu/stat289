Special K

Special K is a brand of breakfast cereal and meal bars manufactured by Kellogg's . The cereal was introduced to the United States in 1955. [1] It is made primarily from grains like lightly toasted rice , wheat and barley . Special K used to be marketed primarily as a low-fat cereal that can be eaten to help one lose weight . [2]

Special K is a lightly toasted breakfast cereal manufactured by the Kellogg Company . It is made with rice and wheat for the European market, but for the North American market it is made without wheat. Special K advertising is heavily marketed towards women, perhaps because in the 1970s it established a reputation as one of the first diet cereals. Kellogg's claims that a diet including Special K cereal can help people lose weight ; it bases this claim on independent research. The Kellogg Company introduced this cereal in 1957 , and it now comes in several varieties.
