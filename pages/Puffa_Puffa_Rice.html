Puffa Puffa Rice

Puffa Puffa Rice was a brand of breakfast cereal produced by the Kellogg Company . Somewhere between Puffed Wheat and Rice Krispies , the product was a form of puffed rice with a sweet, smooth texture and flavoured with brown sugar. Puffa Puffa Rice was introduced in 1967 marketed with a Hawaiian theme and the product shown arriving on a surfboard. [1] In 1972 the box was re-designed to feature a toy steam locomotive. [2] In 1973 it changed again to feature popular puppet character Sooty waving a magician's wand over the cereal. [3] Puffa Puffa Rice was abruptly phased out during 1975.
