Captain Planet and the Planeteers

Captain Planet and the Planeteers is an American animated environmentalist television program created by Ted Turner and Barbara Pyle . [1] The series was produced by Turner Program Services and DiC Animation City and it was broadcast on TBS from September 15, 1990, to December 5, 1992. [2] A sequel series, The New Adventures of Captain Planet , was produced by Hanna-Barbera Cartoons, Inc. and distributed by Turner Program Services and was broadcast from September 11, 1993, to May 11, 1996. [3] Both series continue today in syndication . [4] The program is a form of edutainment and advocates environmentalism and is famous for having a number of famous actors providing voices for the villains. [5] [6] The show spawned a franchise consisting of a charity, a Marvel comic book series, video games, and a TV crossover with OK K.O.! Let's Be Heroes .
