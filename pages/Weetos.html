Weetos

Weetos is a brand of chocolate-flavoured breakfast cereal produced by Weetabix Food Company . The name comes from the fact that its primary ingredient is wheat ( Weet- ) and the cereal pieces are in O shapes ( -Os ), the same naming convention that is used on the company's flagship cereal Weetabix (Wheat Biscuits).

Weetos is a breakfast cereal produced by Weetabix Limited . Its primary ingredient is wheat . The cereal is in O shapes, and is available with chocolate or honey flavouring. In the mid '00s, Weetabix Ltd lowered the amount of sugar present in Weetos as part of a drive to appeal to health conscious parents, which resulted in a slight change of flavour. This was coupled with the dropping of long-running mascot ‘Professor Weeto (Mark Butterworth)’ in favour of neutral images of children participating in various sports. The original mascot was a skateboarder named Derek, who 'found life really boring, until new Weeto's came out of the blue!' delivered in a broad West Midlands accent.
