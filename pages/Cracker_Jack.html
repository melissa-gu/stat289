Cracker Jack

Cracker Jack is an American brand of snack consisting of molasses -flavored, caramel-coated popcorn and peanuts , well known for being packaged with a prize of trivial value inside. The Cracker Jack name was registered in 1896. A slogan, "The More You Eat The More You Want", was also registered that year. [1] Some food historians consider it the first junk food . [2]
