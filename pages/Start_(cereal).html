Start (cereal)

Start (Multi-Grain Start) was a breakfast cereal which was produced by Kellogg's in the UK from the mid-1980s until 2018. Start was promoted as a cereal designed for improving sports performance. It was made from wheat , corn and oats and a single bowlful was said to provide a third of a human's daily vitamin RDA . It was suitable for vegetarians but not for wheat allergy sufferers. Start was discontinued in 2018. [ citation needed ]
