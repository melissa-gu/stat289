Uncle Tobys

Uncle Tobys is an Australian brand of breakfast cereals and other breakfast food products. The brand has a lot of history and is mentioned in an 1892 newspaper. [1] Their main manufacturing base is located in the small town of Wahgunyah , on the NSW / Victorian Border. Following the gold rush in Australia, two brothers Leonard and George Parsons left England and started manufacturing John Bull Oats in Melbourne. [2] In 1861 the Uncle Tobys Company was established, originally under the name of Parsons Bros. [3]
