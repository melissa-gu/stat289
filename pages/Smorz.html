Smorz

Smorz is a breakfast cereal manufactured by the Kellogg Company , consisting of chocolate graham -flavored corn puffs and marshmallows , modeled after the flavor of S'mores . The breakfast cereal was first released in 2003, but was discontinued in December 2013. [1] It was announced in December 2015 that Smorz would be reintroduced, and it can be found in stores again today.
