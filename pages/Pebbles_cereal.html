Pebbles cereal

Cocoa Pebbles and Fruity Pebbles are brands of breakfast cereal introduced by Post Foods in 1971 [1] featuring characters from the animated series The Flintstones as spokestoons . Cocoa Pebbles contains chocolate -flavored crisp rice cereal bits, while Fruity Pebbles contains crisp rice cereal bits that come in a variety of fruit flavors with a sugar content of 9 grams per serving for Fruity Pebbles and 10 grams per serving for Cocoa Pebbles. [2] [3] It is the oldest surviving cereal brand that is based on characters from a TV series or movie. [4]
