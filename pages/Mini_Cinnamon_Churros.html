Mini Cinnamon Churros

Mini Cinnamon Churros is a sweetened wheat breakfast cereal produced by Post Cereals , and based on the churro . [1] The cereal pieces are said to look like inflated cheerios with a heavy dusting of cinnamon and sugar. [2]
