Nintendo Cereal System

Nintendo Cereal System is a breakfast cereal which was produced by Ralston Cereals in 1988 and discontinued in 1989. The name of the cereal was based on the Nintendo Entertainment System , and represents two of the most popular video games for the NES at the time: Super Mario Bros. and The Legend of Zelda . Over the years, the cereal has been sold as memorabilia for collectors on online auction sites, at prices exceeding $100 per box. [3] [4] In 2010, a box was sold on eBay for over $200. [2]
