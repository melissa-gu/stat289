Ice Cream Cones (cereal)

Ice Cream Cones was a breakfast cereal from General Mills that was introduced in 1987. [1] [2] [3] [4] The cereal consisted of puffs sweetened for chocolate , vanilla or chocolate chip ice cream flavor, as well as sugar cone -shaped pieces sweetened for sugar cone flavor. [4] The cereal’s mascot was an animated character named Ice Cream Jones, who delivered the cereal to kids on an old-fashioned pedal bike.
