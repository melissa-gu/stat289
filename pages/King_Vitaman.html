King Vitaman

King Vitaman is a brand of sweetened breakfast cereal produced by Quaker Oats and sold in the United States . The cereal entered the marketplace in 1968, and although it has changed mascots over the years, it is still in production but in very limited quantity according to Quaker Oats .
