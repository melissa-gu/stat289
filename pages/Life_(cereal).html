Life (cereal)

Life , stylized as life® , is a breakfast cereal formerly made solely of whole grain oats [ citation needed ] , but now also containing sugar, corn flour , whole wheat flour , and rice flour . It was introduced in 1961 by the Quaker Oats Company . [1] The cereal is distinguished by its characteristic brown checkered squares, with a finer pattern than Chex cereal.
