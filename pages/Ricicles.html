Ricicles

Ricicles (stylised as RiCiCLES ) is a breakfast cereal sold by Kellogg's in the United Kingdom and Ireland . [1] [2] It is similar to another Kellogg's product, Rice Krispies , but with the addition of a frosted sugar coating. The product is known as Frosted Krispies in the United States.
